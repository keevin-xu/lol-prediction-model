## Model Changes

### Prediction Pipeline (`model/predict.py`)

**Cross-region detector + regional adjustment:**
- Identifies when two teams are from different regions (EU vs PCS, KR vs NA, etc.)
- Applies soloq-derived ELO offset between regions (EU +134, PCS -62, scaled to 80%)
- Region tiers: Tier 1 (KR, CN, EU), Tier 2 (NA, VN, PCS, TR), Tier 3 (BR, JP, OCE, LAS)
- Effect: KC vs DCG went from 47/53 (wrong) to 85/15 (matches market 87/15)

**Blue side offset:**
- +22.3 ELO for blue side, derived from 53.2% blue win rate across 10,372 matches
- Available via `--side blue/red` CLI flag

**Platt calibration:**
- Wired into all predictions automatically
- Fixes overconfident extremes: model said 92% → actual 80%
- Brier improved: 0.2256 → 0.2242
- Parameters: a=0.910, b=0.045 (refitted with blue side offset included)

**Bo3/Bo5/Bo7 series conversion:**
- Negative binomial formula: converts single-game P(win) to series P(win)
- 60% single-game → 64.8% Bo3 → 68.3% Bo5 → 71.0% Bo7

### Kelly Sizing (`polymarket/edge.py`, `polymarket/paper_trader.py`)
- Reduced from 25% to 6.25% (quarter-Kelly)
- Max position size: 6.25% of bankroll
- Max bet on $1,000 bankroll: $62.50

### Calibration (`model/calibration.py`)
- Fitted on 8,888 walk-forward backtest predictions
- Applied at prediction time (not just analysis)

## Polymarket Scanner Fixes (`polymarket/scanner.py`)

**Three bugs fixed:**
1. Used `tag_slug=league-of-legends` instead of paginating 2000+ generic events — finds all LoL markets including buried ones
2. `outcomePrices` comes as JSON string from Gamma API, not a list — was silently failing on `float('[')`
3. Team name parsing failed on `"LoL: X vs Y (BO5) - Tournament"` — added `_clean_team_name()` to strip prefixes/suffixes

**Filters added:**
- Skip resolved markets (price >= 99% or <= 1%)
- Skip per-game sub-markets (Game 1/2/3/4/5 winners)
- Skip handicap and over/under props
- Deduplicate: one opportunity per event slug per team pair

**Result:** 0 tradeable markets → 1 clean market (KC vs DCG 86/14)

## Key Discovery: CLOB Price History

The Polymarket CLOB API returns price history for resolved markets when called with `startTs=1`:
```
GET https://clob.polymarket.com/prices-history?market={token_id}&startTs=1&fidelity=100
```
Without `startTs`, it returns empty. This enables retroactive analysis of all 486+ resolved T2 markets.

## UPDATED FINDING: Model Beats Polymarket at Market Open

The "96% closing accuracy" was a measurement artifact — closing prices include in-game information.

**Pre-match vs closing efficiency on 100 resolved T2 markets (10-min resolution):**
```
                Model    PM Open   PM Mid    PM 75%   PM Close
Accuracy        67.0%     61.0%     68.0%     81.0%    100.0%
Brier           0.2011    0.2178    0.1880    0.1418    0.0000
```

**The model BEATS Polymarket at market open** (Brier 0.2011 < 0.2178, accuracy 67% > 61%).

Opening lines are soft — 61% accurate with average price drift of 0.444 from open to close.

**Strategy: bet at market open, before sharp money arrives.**

V3 model (107 features including objectives, draft, vision, economy) did not improve accuracy over V1 (63.4% vs 63.8%) — confirming 63-64% is the pre-match ceiling from public data. But that ceiling beats the opening line.

## What This Means

1. **The model has edge at market open.** Deploy against opening lines, not closing.
2. **V3 features (107) don't help accuracy** — the ceiling is ~64% from pre-match public data.
3. **The infrastructure is correct and ready** — scanner, price tracker, bot, backtester all work.
4. **Next step: automate early betting** — detect new market creation, compare model vs opening price, bet within the first hours before the line hardens.
