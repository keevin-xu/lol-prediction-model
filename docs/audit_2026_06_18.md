## Missing Guardrail (flagged, not implemented)

**ELO-staleness suppressor:** The Galions problem — a team whose recent results diverge sharply from their ELO (5 of top 10 confident losses). The model underestimates teams on hot streaks and overestimates teams on cold streaks that ELO hasn't caught up to.

Recommended: compare model probability against the team's recent 5-game win rate. If the discrepancy is >20% (model says 30% but team won 4 of last 5), de-size or suppress. **Not implemented — flagging only.**
